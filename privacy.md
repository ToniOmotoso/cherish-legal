# Privacy Policy for Cherish

**Last updated:** June 21, 2026

## TL;DR

Cherish is a friendship-tracking app that stores everything **locally on your device**. We don't have a server. We don't collect, see, or sell your data. We don't track you. We don't show ads.

This privacy policy explains what that means in detail.

---

## What information Cherish collects

**None that leaves your phone.**

When you use Cherish, you create:

- Friends (names, photos, notes, tags, birthdays, etc.)
- Events (birthdays, anniversaries, milestones)
- Hangouts (your own scheduled events)
- Self-reminders ("call Peter", "take vitamins")
- Saved gift ideas
- A "Me" profile with your name

All of this data is stored on your iPhone using iOS's local storage (AsyncStorage and the app's documents directory). **It is never sent to a server we control.** We do not have a server. We have no way to see, retrieve, or modify your data.

## Photos

Cherish lets you add photos to friend profiles. These photos are stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded anywhere.

## Notifications

Cherish schedules notifications locally on your device using iOS notification APIs. Notifications are scheduled and delivered by your iPhone itself, not by us. We don't see when or whether your notifications fire.

## Location data

Cherish lets you add a location to events using Google Places autocomplete. Your search queries go to Google's Places API to fetch suggestions. We do not store, log, or see these queries. We do not track your location.

If you tap a location to open it in Google Maps, that's a normal link that opens Maps — we don't intercept or log it.

## Backups

Cherish writes a backup file to your device's documents folder. If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iPhone (most users do), this file is included in your iCloud backup so your data survives restoring to a new phone. **The backup goes from your phone to your iCloud account directly — we never see it.**

You can also manually export your data as a JSON file from Settings → Your data. That file goes wherever you choose to save it (Files, email, etc.) — we never see it.

## Third-party services

Cherish uses:

- **Google Places API** (for location autocomplete) — your search queries go to Google. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com.
- **Apple's iOS Notification System** — your phone's own notification scheduling.
- **iCloud Backup** (if enabled in your iOS Settings) — handled by Apple.

We have no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting that contains personal data, no third-party trackers.

## Data we receive

We receive nothing. Cherish does not phone home. There is no account system. We don't have your email, your name, your data, or anything else.

## Children's privacy

Cherish is rated 4+ but is not specifically directed at children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect information from children either.

## Changes to this policy

If we add cloud sync, accounts, or any data collection in future versions, this privacy policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Material changes will be communicated through the App Store listing.
