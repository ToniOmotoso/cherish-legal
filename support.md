## Frequently asked questions

### Where is my data stored?

Everything you add to Cherish — friends, events, hangouts, reminders, photos — is stored **locally on your iPhone**. Cherish doesn't have a server, doesn't have accounts, and doesn't send your data anywhere.

### What happens if I get a new phone?

If you restore your new iPhone from an iCloud backup, your Cherish data comes with it automatically. iOS includes Cherish's documents folder in iCloud Backup by default.

If you want extra safety, you can also export your data manually:

1. Open Cherish
2. Go to **Settings → Your data → Export my data**
3. Save the file to iCloud Drive, email it to yourself, or AirDrop it to your new device
4. On the new device, install Cherish, open Settings → Your data → **Import data** → pick the file

### My notifications aren't working

1. Check that notifications are enabled in iOS: **Settings → Cherish → Notifications**
2. Inside Cherish, go to **Settings → Notifications → Check Permissions** to re-prompt
3. If you're seeing notifications you don't recognize (or notifications from friends you've deleted), tap **Settings → Notifications → Reset notifications** to clear everything and start fresh

### How do I delete a friend / event / reminder?

- **Friend:** Open their profile → scroll to the bottom → **Delete friend**. Or swipe left on a friend in the Friends tab.
- **Event:** Tap the event from a friend profile or from Home → tap the trash icon.
- **Hangout:** Tap the hangout → tap the trash icon.
- **Self-reminder:** Tap the reminder from the Me tab → tap **Delete**.

When you delete anything, Cherish automatically cancels any scheduled notifications for it.

### How do gift ideas work?

Gift ideas are a curated bank of 120+ suggestions across 15 categories. Each suggestion is scored based on the friend's tags, interests, and optional gender field. To see ideas for a friend:

1. Open their profile
2. Tap the yellow **"Gift ideas for [Name] 🎁"** card
3. Tap any suggestion to mark it as a favorite — saved gifts appear at the top
4. Use the filter chips (Cozy, Foodie, Splurge, Practical, Experience) to narrow down

### Why does Cherish ask for location access?

Cherish doesn't track your location. It does use Google Places to autocomplete addresses when you add a location to a hangout or event. Search queries go to Google's API — we don't see them.

### Can I edit my own name?

Yes. Go to the **Me** tab. Tap **Edit**. Change anything in your profile — name, avatar color, tags, etc.

### How do I report a bug?

Email us at [infocherishtheapp@gmail.com] with:

- What you were doing when it happened
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- A screenshot if you have one
- Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About on iOS)
